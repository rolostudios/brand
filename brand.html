Brand Guidelines

RoloStudios is a design focused agency. We are a strategic nearshore production partner. We are agile, built to scale and priced to white label. We focus on crafting beautiful digital experiences that grow, engage and convert your audience.

Logo

Our logo represents what RoloStudios stands for - creating slick digital visual experiences. We’ve put the “R” and “S” letters uppercase to later use those as our symbol, we also modified the first and last “o” in the name to show open and close brackets making a subtle reference to coding.

Typography and Layout

Our tipography reflects a modern aproach for the brand. the round geometry communicates an everlasting evolution, it has to be used for every brand communication so it keeps consistency all across every media.

Color

Color at RoloStudios is very important to keep the brand's consistency, we use red to reflect our passion and compromise with every project and to keep the human side always present, its warm and affective, it defines us.
